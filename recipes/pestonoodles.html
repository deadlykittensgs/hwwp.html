Pesto noodles

The perfect pesto noodles

noodles with pesto has been one of my main sources of food and i would like to share it with you

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • garlic
  • pesto
  • parmesan cheese
  • spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • water

Instructions

  1. boil water
  2. Cook the noodles once water boils
  3. drain water when noodles finish boiling
  4. add pesto and all other spices and stir
  5. top with cheese and garlic
  6. let rest before serving
