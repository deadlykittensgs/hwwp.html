PB&J With Bananas

The perfect PB&J with banana

Peanut Butter and jelly sandwiches are good but they are much better with banana. today i will tell you how to make this delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly
  • A banana
  • Nutella (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put all desired spreadables on bread
  2. slice banana
  3. put bannana slices on bread
  4. close sandwich
  5. put on griddle and warm both sides
  6. let rest before serving
