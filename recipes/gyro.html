Gyros

The Perfect Gyro

The Gyro is the main food of the people in Athens. It is a simple sandwich normally made with pork. here is a quick recipe to make one on your own

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato slices
  • Taziki
  • spices and seasoning
  • Pita bread
  • pork

Instructions

  1. Make the meat
  2. toast the pita bread
  3. put tiziki sause on the pita bread
  4. put meat onions and tomatos on the pita bread
  5. sprinkle with spices
  6. wrap and enjoy
